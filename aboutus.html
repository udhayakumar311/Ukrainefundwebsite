About Us


Care Club is a not-for-profit NGO which has been working for the welfare and well being of people not only in India but worldwide. Care Club has set up a network of volunteers spread across the globe who checks the needs of the poor and needy on daily basis through its volunteers’ network.


As Russia invades Ukraine, Care Club is committed to helping the individuals and families seeking shelter, security, and peace. As the military invasion in Ukraine escalates, millions of people are facing a serious humanitarian crisis and are in need of urgent assistance. Together, we can support communities impacted by this devastating violence and the regions open to helping displaced people.


Every donator is assured that their funds are delivered to the correct recipients. We’re also verifying that donors and organizers are acting in compliance with US and international laws, including global financial laws and regulations and evolving economic sanctions. All donations raised will be distributed to verified nonprofit organizations supporting vulnerable communities to obtain access to shelter, food, medical services, education, and psychosocial support, as well as other people impacted.


For questions about fundraising on Ukraine relief, view the Contact US page and ask your questions and doubts. Donate to Ukraine relief efforts today to help the people of Ukraine in this time of need.
