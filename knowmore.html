We believe that we can save more lives with you !

SHELTERS

The money collected is used for providing shelters to the people who has lost their homes in the war

FOOD & WATER

One fourth of the money collected is used for providing food and water to the refugees and orphans

HEALTH CARE

One part of the donation is used for hospital and healthcare services for treating the affected

TRANSPORT

Fund collected is used for transporting the people from affected places to the refugee camps and to the places required

PRIMARY EDUCATION

Fund collected is also used for providing education temporarily to the students and people in the shelter camps

ELECTRICITY

Due to the war attacks, most of the places has lost their normal connectivity to cell phone networks and electricity. Funds collected are used to provide temporary electricity supplies to affected areas and shelter camps.
